Welkom op Natuurpraktijk Jacobs

U kunt hier terecht voor uw medische en medisch-psychische zorgvragen. Mijn naam is Jaap Jacobs, arts en eigenaar van Natuurpraktijk Jacobs. Ik begeleid en behandel volgens de integrale geneeskunde. Integrale geneeskunde behelst de combinatie van reguliere gezondheidszorg met, bij Natuurpraktijk Jacobs, methodes van orthomoleculaire geneeswijze, natuurgeneeswijze en acupunctuur. Hierdoor verminderen meestal uw klachten, komt u nader bij uzelf en gaat u bewuster, vitaler en vrijer leven.

WHAT DRIVES US

We're passionate about building a better meeting workflow

Our calendar software company was founded with the goal of helping people better manage their time and stay organized. We recognized that many people struggle to keep track of their busy schedules, and we wanted to provide a simple and effective solution. With our calendar software, we've created an easy-to-use tool that allows users to quickly add and view events, set reminders, and invite others to events. We've also made sure to integrate seamlessly with popular email services, so that users can easily stay connected with their colleagues and friends.
